01 — Executive Summary

The Gap Between Scan and Ship

Core Finding

Every competitor in this space does one of two things: they scan your code and hand you a report, or they charge $150/hr to fix it for you. Nobody autonomously fixes the code and gives you a deploy-ready artifact. That gap — between "here's what's wrong" to "here's your hardened codebase + deployment pipeline" — is wide open. V2P owns it.

The vibe-to-production market is fragmenting into four lanes: scan-only tools (Aikido, ZeriFlow, Vibe Check, Vibe App Scanner), consulting services (Vibe Code Clean, Synergy Labs, Network Thinking), platform lock-in (Retool, Replit, Lovable), and pen-testing OSS (VibePenTester, VibeSecurity GitHub). Every lane has the same structural weakness: they tell you what's broken but leave you to figure out how to fix it — or they fix it manually at consulting rates. None of them provide the autonomous fix loop + production deployment templates that V2P delivers.

The 45% stat from Synergy Labs — that nearly half of AI-generated code contains security vulnerabilities — is the fear hook that drives the entire market. But fear without a one-command resolution path just creates paralysis. V2P converts that paralysis into action: scan → fix → deploy, overnight, autonomously.
